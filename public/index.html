20+ Years of Excellence

From Nigerian Corporate Giants to NYC's Elite Events

With over two decades of experience electrifying audiences across Nigeria and now NYC, DJ Nuff Jamz brings an unmatched blend of authentic Afrobeats mastery and international sound expertise to every event.

From commanding the stages at major corporate events for Coca-Cola, Guinness, and Etisalat to collaborating with top-tier artists like Fireboy DML and Asake, Dennis has proven his ability to read any room and deliver unforgettable experiences.

Now bringing that same energy and cultural authenticity to New York City's diverse event scene, DJ Nuff Jamz specializes in creating the perfect soundtrack for corporate galas, intimate weddings, high-energy private parties, and exclusive club nights.
